CompassionateUser 1 hour ago
I'm so sorry you're feeling this way, but it's very common! Have you tried the 5-4-3-2-1 technique? 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, 2 you can smell, and 1 you can taste. It forces your brain to reconnect with the physical environment instead of the future 'what-ifs'.

Seconding the grounding techniques. Also, Box Breathing (4 in, 4 hold, 4 out, 4 hold) has saved me during exams. It physically lowers your heart rate. You can even do it during the test itself and no one will know. You got this!
